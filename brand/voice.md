# Brand voice

## Coopr (the agent)
Speaks like a producer reading the room — confident, dry, useful. Not a chatbot.

**Yes:**
- "Tightened hook 3 — kept the picks alone."
- "Eight-breaths phrase should land twice."
- "Indexed for retrieval — won't shape it unless asked."

**No:**
- "Hi there! 👋 I'd love to help you with that!"
- "Great question! Let me break this down for you..."
- "I've taken the liberty of..."
- "Hope this helps!"

## Body copy / UI strings

**Tone:** dry-witty when it lands the punchline; otherwise functional. No filler. No marketing.

**Yes:**
- "Drafting · v3"
- "Schedule approval only"
- "Trial reel A · 420 words"
- "Coopr · 14m ago · three edits — hook tightened, two shots added, caption drafted"

**No:**
- "🎉 Awesome! You drafted v3!"
- "Let's get started on your amazing content!"
- Any exclamation point unless it's a literal data quote
- Em-dashes in editorial copy are fine; em-dashes as "AI tells"  are not

## Headlines

Editorial italic. Lyrical second line completes the headline.

**Reference (Insights/Overview surface):**
> "+22% saves, +12% views. *One channel is going backwards.*"

The italic continuation is what makes a COOPR headline feel editorial vs. dashboard.

## Eyebrows + datelines

Mono caps, letter-spacing 0.10–0.12em. Mono regular for datelines.

```
INSIGHTS · OVERVIEW · LAST 30 DAYS         3 PLATFORMS · 47 POSTS INDEXED
SINCE MON 09:00 · +1.4K FOLLOWERS                                  4D AGO
```

## Numbers

Always tabular mono. Never lining figures in serif.

**Yes:** `1,842 / 2,400` · `+340% saves` · `00:18`
**No:** `1,842 / 2,400` in Literata · spelled-out numbers in UI

## Empty states

Italic serif placeholder, fg-tertiary. Direct, not chirpy.

**Yes:** "Start writing." · "No comments yet." · "Indexed for retrieval — won't shape it unless asked."
**No:** "Looks like there's nothing here yet! Let's create something amazing 🚀"
